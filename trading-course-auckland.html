Professional Certificate in Technical Analysis: Auckland

Status: Open for Enrollment 2025

The Auckland regional cohort provides traders with the institutional skills required for modern crypto and forex markets. This curriculum is tailored for residents of New Zealand seeking professional certification in technical analysis.

Module Syllabus (Auckland Edition)

  • UNIT 101 Exchange Infrastructure & Wallet Security
  • UNIT 102 Advanced Charting (RSI, MACD, Fibonacci)
  • UNIT 103 Risk Management Protocols for New Zealand Markets
  • UNIT 104 Derivatives & Leverage Trading Strategies

Required Trading Practicum

To access the course materials and execute live market analysis, students must register a verified practice terminal with our technology partner.
